How it works together

The research layer, end to end

The Terminal modules are designed to stack. Here's what a single automated research workflow looks like when the series work in combination.

🏛️
Economic Indicators

CPI release in 2 days — pre-release brief fires

Your Economic Indicators agent delivers the consensus estimate, prior month's reading, and Fed 2% target gap. You know what's coming before the market open.

📈
Options Trading

IV spikes on SPY/QQQ — flow alert fires

Your options flow agent detects unusual volume on index options. IV rank crosses 60. The pre-market scanner shows elevated OI at key strikes. You see the positioning, not just the price.

📄
Corporate Filings

8-K filed — material event detected before headlines

A company on your watchlist files an 8-K at 5:47 AM. Your EDGAR agent parses the filing type, flags it as material, and delivers the summary before it hits financial news.

📊
Risk & Portfolio — In Development

Position risk re-evaluated against new macro environment

Your portfolio risk agent recalculates beta and correlation across your held positions given the new CPI data. Concentration alerts fire if any single macro factor now dominates your exposure.

🌅
All modules

Morning brief delivered — you trade, not research

By 8:50 AM your complete research brief is waiting. Macro context, options positioning, relevant filings, portfolio risk delta. You open your laptop informed, not scrambling.
