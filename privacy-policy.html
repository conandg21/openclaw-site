Privacy Policy

Plain English — what we collect, what we don't, and how we handle your information.

Last updated: March 2026

Short version: learnopenclaw.org is a static informational website. We don't run analytics, we don't track you, and we don't sell your data. The only personal information we collect is your email address if you voluntarily subscribe to our newsletter.

Who we are

learnopenclaw.org is an independent community learning resource about the OpenClaw AI agent framework. This site is not affiliated with or endorsed by the official OpenClaw project or Peter Steinberger.

If you have questions about this policy, you can reach us at hello@learnopenclaw.org.

What data we collect

We collect the minimum possible. Specifically:

  • Email address — only if you voluntarily subscribe to our newsletter using the signup form on the site. We use ConvertKit to manage subscriptions. Your email is never sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Standard server logs — like most websites, our hosting provider may log basic request information such as your IP address, browser type, and the pages you visit. These logs are used only for security and operational purposes and are not used to identify or track individuals.

We do not collect:

  • Analytics or behavioral tracking (no Google Analytics, no tracking pixels)
  • Cookies beyond what is strictly necessary
  • Payment information (this site has no paid features)
  • Account information (there are no user accounts on this site)

Newsletter (ConvertKit)

If you subscribe to our newsletter, your email address is stored and processed by ConvertKit (now called Kit). ConvertKit is a third-party email service provider. You can read their privacy policy at kit.com/privacy.

We use your email address only to send you updates about OpenClaw. We will never spam you or share your email with other parties.

You can unsubscribe at any time using the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any email we send.

Third-party links

This site links to third-party websites including GitHub, openclaw.ai, and others. Once you leave learnopenclaw.org, this privacy policy no longer applies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any sites you visit.

Search

The search feature on this site runs entirely in your browser using Lunr.js. Your search queries are never sent to any server — all searching happens locally on your device.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us so we can delete it.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete information we hold about you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@learnopenclaw.org.

If you are located in the European Union, you have rights under the GDPR. If you are a California resident, you have rights under the CCPA. We are happy to honor any requests under applicable law.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please email us at hello@learnopenclaw.org.
