What is it?

Not a chatbot. An agent.

A chatbot answers questions. OpenClaw takes action — on your computer, in your apps, on your behalf. Here's what makes it different.

🧠

It reasons, then acts

OpenClaw uses a large language model (like Claude or GPT-4) as its brain. You give it a goal in plain English, and it figures out the steps — then carries them out, one by one.

💬

Works in apps you already use

No new interface to learn. Talk to your agent through WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack, Discord, or iMessage — wherever you already spend your time.

🔒

Runs on your machine

OpenClaw lives on your computer, not in the cloud. Your files, emails, and data stay with you. You can even run it completely offline using a local AI model — no API key needed.

🆓

Completely free

Open-source and free forever. Bring your own API key for a cloud model, or use a local model at no cost at all. No subscriptions, no vendor lock-in, no hidden fees.

What it can do

Real tasks. Real results.

OpenClaw bridges the gap between AI intelligence and real-world action. Tell it what you need — it handles the how.

📧

Manage your email

Summarise your inbox, draft replies, and set up automated email workflows — all from a chat message.

📅

Handle your calendar

Schedule meetings, check availability, set reminders — through natural conversation, not a dashboard.

🌐

Browse the web

Search, extract information, and compile research from the internet on demand.

📁

Work with files

Create, edit, organise, and summarise files on your computer using plain-language instructions.

🤖

Your choice of AI model

Works with Claude, GPT-4, Gemini, or a fully local model like LLaMA. Switch any time with a single config change.

🧩

100+ agent skills

Extend your agent with skills from ClawHub — Slack, Shopify, GitHub, web automation, and much more.
